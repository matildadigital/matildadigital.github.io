TapMeasure Help

Occipital launched the TapMeasure app, a ARkit ready app for measuring spaces with your iPhone. I built this responsive, mobile first Help page that imports FAQ content written by our customer experience team. The design takes advantage of Flexbox to scale well for multiple devices.

Bridge Headset for iPhone 8

Occipital's Bridge headset is compatible with multiple iPhone versions and takes advantage of its many performance improvements. I built this responsive announcement page to showcase its awesome features. Some great Flexbox uses also help this page scale well and it integrates the purchase options with our store at Structure.io's website through Django.
