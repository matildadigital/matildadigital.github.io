We sell brackets for the different iPad models our device works with and some brackets work with multiple models. To help clarify the multi-device compatibility and track sales of these brackets, I built a modal that routes the correct product choice to our store cart through our Django system. IoT with ESP8266

The ESP8266 is an inexpensive WiFi enabled microcontroller. It's useful for IoT projects. At Make Santa Fe during an IoY workshop, I learned how to program one to send messages after sensing an input and to produce and output from Blynk, an app for creating interfaces for IoT objects.
