UX Designer

Creatively solving communication problems is at the heart of my fascination with technology. Designing digital products gives me the opportunity to devise just the right form and structure to help people understand the digital systems around them. I see design as a means to welcome, connect with, and guide people through technology.

Bridge Headset Apps Collection

Occipital's Bridge Headset powered by Structure Sensor has an increasing number of available apps. Their product website needed an evergreen design that could transform into more of an apps store. After researching current solutions and customer needs, I created this UI design.

Aerospace Alliance App

The Aerospace Alliance serves to establish the Southeast region as a world-class aerospace and aviation corridor. Business leaders interested in working with Alliance companies needed to easily understand them and their products and services. I designed this app to replace the bulky catalogs that previously filled this need.

Introduction to Python Course

The Arapahoe Libraries offered free Treehouse coding course subscriptions to its users. I took advantage of this opportunity to see how we could support adult learners as they explore software development. After 3 iterations, I created a popular, sold-out class.
