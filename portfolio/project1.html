Motto Mortgage's interaction and visual design for MottoCenter intranet search

About This Project

Client: Motto Mortgage (Motto Franchising, LLC)

  • Mortgage loan franchisor
  • Generates income from franchise fees
  • Provides MottoCenter web application to franchisees
  • Company at Level 1 of the InVision Design Maturity Model

Busines Problem and Target User

Re-design the search results page within MottoCenter to display more search results above the fold for brokers, loan orignators, and other franchise staff.

Team and Role

Role: UX/UI designer
Stakeholders:
Product Manager
VP of Product and Strategy

Constraints and Scope

  • User research prohibited by primary stakeholder
  • No available analytics data
  • Two week Agile Scrum sprint

Research

  • Informal competitive analysis
  • Examined designs of searches for informational/resource content, not e-commerce
  • Primary takeaway: include images of content when they are unique and can be used to quickly scan and identify desired content. Otherwise, emphasize relevancy of excerpt.

New York Public Library catalog search

New York Times search

Oreilly media search

Washington Post search

Wikipedia search

Design Approach

  • Depart from card-style layout - reduce vertical space of search result
  • Omit images - provides no valuable information to pinpoint desired content

After

Outcomes

  • Increased search results above fold from 3 to 5
  • Recommended user testing to validate usability of new design for customers
  • Recommended Google Analytics to track usage and interaction with new UI elements
