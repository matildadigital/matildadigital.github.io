Meet Matilda

Profile

Matilda Asuzu is a tactical, pragmatic user experience designer. She focuses on understanding a company's business goals and processes and charting a tailored course for achieving tangible value from UX design practices in their product development. She works at RE/MAX, LLC designing web applications that support 80K+ real estate agents across the US as well as marketing websites that drive lead generation for RE/MAX franchise owners. Outside work, she enjoys discussing books on UX business practices at UX Book Club Denver and trains for long distance running.
